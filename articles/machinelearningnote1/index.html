1. Markov Chains

  • Irreducible

    We can access any states from any states. $Pr(x_i=s|x_j=k)>0$

  • Aperiodic

    Transition matrix has non-zero diagonal.$Pr(x_i=s|x_{i-1}=s)>0$

  • Stationary

    $P_{s->t}$ is independent of n.

  • Unique Equilibrium

    i) Stationary
    ii) Irreducible
    iii) Aperiodic

  • Conditional Independent

    $Pr(x,y|z)=Pr(x|z)Pr(y|z)$
