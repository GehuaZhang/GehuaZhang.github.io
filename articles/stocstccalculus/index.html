Stochastic Calculus

Brownian Motion

1. Standard Brownian Motion = Wiener Process

1) $W_0=0$

2) $W_{t_n}-W_{t_k} \sim N(0,t_n-t_k)$

3) Independent increments

2. Geometric Brownian Motion

$$dS_t = S_{t_n}-S_{t_{n-1}} = \mu S_t dt + \sigma S_t \sqrt{dt}$$

Since $dW_t = W_{t_n}-W_{t_k} \sim N(0,dt)$, we say $dW_t=\sqrt{dt}$, plug in:

\begin{equation}

$$dS_t=\mu S_t dt + \sigma S_t dW_t$$

\end{equation}
